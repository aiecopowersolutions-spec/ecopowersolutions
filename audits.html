Safety & Combustion

Gas sniffers, ambient CO checks, and combustion testing for furnaces, boilers, and water heaters.

  • Appliance backdraft and exhaust verification

Leaks & Drafts

Air, water, and gas leaks in attics, basements, crawlspaces, and around doors and windows.

  • Blower door testing (where applicable)

Insulation & Attic

Insulation levels, attic conditions, and ventilation that affect comfort and mold risk.

  • Attic and knee wall insulation
  • Air sealing opportunities

HVAC & Ventilation

Heaters, AC units, thermostats, bath fans, and exhaust systems that move air through your home.

  • Equipment condition and basic performance
  • Exhaust and ventilation checks

Electrical & Piping

Visible wiring, panels, and piping issues that may indicate safety or moisture problems.

  • Noting visible electrical red flags
  • Checking for rust, corrosion, and leaks

Appliances & Programs

Major appliances and equipment that may qualify for swap programs or efficiency incentives.

  • Appliance efficiency and age review
  • Identifying potential rebate-covered upgrades

Note: EcoPower audits are assessment-only. We do not perform on-the-spot repairs during the audit visit. Our job is to test, document, and guide your next steps.

Hidden Risks

Problems We Aim to Catch Early

Our audits are designed to find issues before they turn into costly emergencies or safety hazards.

CO & Combustion Hazards

Poor venting, cracked heat exchangers, backdrafting, and other issues that can put your household at risk.

Ignored: emergency calls, health risks, property damage

Moisture & Water Issues

Leaks, condensation, and humidity problems that can lead to mold, rot, and structural damage over time.

Ignored: mold cleanup, drywall & flooring replacement

Drafts & Energy Waste

Gaps, air leaks, and insulation failures that make your systems work harder and cost you more every month.

Ignored: higher bills, shorter equipment life
