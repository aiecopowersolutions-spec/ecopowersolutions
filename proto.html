BGE Energy Efficiency Participating Service Provider

We help turn qualifying improvements into real rebate opportunities.

EcoPower Solutions is a BGE Energy Efficiency Participating Service Provider. We connect whole-home audit data, recommended improvements, program-approved energy modeling, required documentation, and qualifying installation work so eligible customers can pursue incentives available through BGE Energy Efficiency.

Start With an Energy Audit Explore BGE Home Performance

A built-in advantage over an audit-only or installation-only contractor

  • Audit findings are translated into a practical improvement scope.
  • Program-approved modeling estimates energy savings and potential incentives available through BGE Energy Efficiency.
  • Eligible upgrades can be coordinated across insulation, air sealing, HVAC, electrification, water heating, ventilation, and weatherization.
  • EcoPower can complete the selected work and support required testing and program documentation.

Eligibility and incentive amounts depend on the customer account, property, selected measures, modeled savings, project cost, installation requirements, available funding, and current BGE Energy Efficiency program rules. Participation does not imply BGE endorsement, and incentives are not guaranteed.
